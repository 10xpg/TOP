Mash Kenkey

Description

Ingredients

  • Kenkey
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Peanut Butter
  • Water
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Cut kenkey into small cubes
  2. Pour kenkey into a blender
  3. Add some water, ice cubes, some peanut butter
  4. Blend until smooth
  5. Serve in a bowl
  6. Add some milk and sugar
