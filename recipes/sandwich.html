Sandwich

Description

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Onions
  • Bell Pepper
  • Powdered Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash your hands
  2. Wash all vegetables
  3. Chop vegetables into favorable sizes
  4. Heat some oil in frying pan
  5. Break and beat your eggs
  6. Add salt to eggs
  7. Add vegetables to eggs
  8. Pour egg mixture into frying pan
  9. Allow to fry for 3 minutes
  10. Turn over to the other side
  11. Remove egg from pan and replace with some butter
  12. Sandwich the egg between 2 slices of bread
  13. Toast the bread and egg sandwich
